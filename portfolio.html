Tech Projects

Here are some of the projects that I work with on a regular basis. Take a look, and let me know what you think.

SLCC Website

I was a big player in updating the SLCC website. I spear-headed the development and did a lot of the research and groundwork during the process. I helped an A+ team drive this one out of the park! Visit the site and check it out today!

Evergreen-ILS

McCaslin Library has been a user of Evergreen software for quite some time. As IT Director, maintenance and upkeep of the software falls on me. I try to contribute back whenever I can (most of the time it's documentation). Learn more about Evergreen here.

YNAB & Linux

It's not a "project," per se, but I've helped contribute a little bit here and there to the Linux install script for YNAB (which I love) on Linux (which I also love) using WINE. Check out my fork here!

Submerge

SLCC is hosting a conference for High School students in September 2015. In preparation, I've been working on getting a website up and running for providing information, and enabling registration. Check it out here!
